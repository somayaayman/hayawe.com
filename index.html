About us

Hayawe WPC products are made from natural fibers,polymers and specialty additives processed.

Hayawe Bio-Wood Manufacturing Co. is an Egyptian company which has been established in 2010

Hayawe Bio-Wood Manufacturing Co. is an Egyptian company which has been established in 2010 with Egyptian German Saudi investments by professionals who have years of experience in the engineering bio-wood manufacturing, besides using the latest technology and the best quality material. Our factory in - Alsadat city - is considered the first factory for Bio-wood production in Egypt and the middle east. Our mission is to provide friendly environmentally wooden product which is considered the best alternative to natural wood with excellent service and high quality to our valued business customers, which made of the best natures wood products and chemically treated plastics. Hayawe produces WPC (Wood Plastic Composite) which is used in many Profiles such as Decking, Fencing, Balcony, Bridges, Car sheds, Cladding, Doors, Out-door Furniture, out-doors landscape and submarine

Hayawe WPC products are made from natural fibers,polymers and specialty additives processed through high temperatures and pressures that are exerted via a digitally controlled extrusion process. Hayawe's WPC offers all the beauty of a natural wood in Environmentally friendly style.
